🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

1. No Data Collection

PromptShield does not collect, transmit, or store any user content on any server.

2. Local Storage Only

The following data may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Application preferences (theme preference)
  • Local analytics (cleanup counts - stored on your device only)

3. Clipboard Access

Clipboard access occurs ONLY when you explicitly click:

  • "Paste" button
  • "Copy" button

4. No Third Parties

PromptShield does not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, third-party analytics, or cloud services.

5. Your Data Stays Yours

All text you paste into PromptShield remains on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
